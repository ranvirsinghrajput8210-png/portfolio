About

A decade of global marketing leadership

Hi, I'm Sukriti — a global marketing leader, digital events strategist, and adjunct professor of digital marketing at Golden Gate University.

With 10+ years across Atlassian, Google Cloud, AMA, and high-growth SaaS companies, I've built a career focused on helping brands grow and helping marketing professionals elevate their stories.

I'm known for designing high-impact marketing programs, scaling global digital events, and creating content strategies that move business results. Over the last decade, I've led hundreds of webinars, virtual summits, and digital experiences across AI, cloud, and B2B marketing.

I specialize in helping marketing professionals articulate their value, build authentic personal brands, and move confidently into their next roles. Because I've lived inside marketing teams for years — from content and demand gen to events, email, and audience acquisition — I know exactly what hiring managers look for.

At the American Marketing Association Bay Area, I serve as a Board Member and lead initiatives that bring the marketing community together. Mentorship, community, and helping professionals find clarity in their careers are some of my biggest passions.
